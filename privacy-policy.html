DealTracker Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Data Collection

DealTracker does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All deal tracking data you enter is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to any server.

Network Requests

The app loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). No user data is included in these requests. No analytics or tracking libraries are used.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the developer via the App Store support link.
